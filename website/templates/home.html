online clearance form

about us

This is an online clearance system that helps students to clear from different departments that they are required to clear from in order to be able to clear from the school and get there certificate.

It will help save time and resources for both the student and school management.It is also going in accordance with the goal of the school being more online oriented.

The departments that student need to clear from include :-

  1. Head of department
  2. Dean of school
  3. University library
  4. University Accommodations Section
  5. Catering Section
  6. Health Unit
  7. Games and Sports Office
  8. Dean of Students
  9. Central Services
  10. Student's finance
  11. Registrar
  12. Financial officer

The financial officer is the one who has the admin authorization of adding and removing students to the clearance form database that contains data of students and if/not they have a balance with the school.
